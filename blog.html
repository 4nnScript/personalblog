SMA Negeri 3 Malang.

I am going to start from high school as believe me, my formal education story is not that amusing. Anyway, in high school I was not a very active student. SMAN 3 Malang, or we call it Bhawikarsu is the best high school in Malang, it is well known with the intellectual students both academic, and non academic. However, I decided to just enjoy my high school time by hanging out with friends, and studying (lol) to make sure I have a strong foundation of knowledge for the higher education. Even so, I still spared my times to join clubs I love such as japanese club and english club.

The first picture was me and Ryouhei-san, he is Japanese who visited our Japanese club. He did a little presentation about his country. On the next picture, it was taken on 2018 when we just finished performing dance class in PCSC event. PSCS is the biggest event in Bhawikarsu, and my class was chosen to perform in it by doing some theatrical dancing with jugle theme! It was so fun, we trained almost everyday after school until 10pm then I finally got fever :) I know. Great way to ruin the final performance Nisa! However, we still got a great applauds, it feels amazing to be on the stage.

Achievements

I am greatful that I have been finding many fortunes in life

EDUACTION Essay East Java Competition

27 September 2020 by KBMPTI FILKOM UB

This was actually the first competition I tried joining during college. My friend and I was just doing it for fun, trying out our luck. We were making idea of an application integrated with AI to boost users motivation to study. A funny story, D-1 we were staying up so late to finish our presentation materials. I went to sleep at 1am and we had to be present in Zoom Meeting at 7am. Stupidly, I fell asleep until 8 am :( My team mate contacted allllll my family member, she even video call my sister through instagram... It was really horrible but somehow we made it through :)

UT INTECH Innovation National Competition

6 Desember 2021 by United Tractor member of ASTRA

I would say this competition worth the effort. We were proposing a website for hospitals and PMI to support demand of convalesent plasma. We proposed this idea for PKM, and we stopped in "pendanaan" phase. However, we see the potential of this idea, so we register it through BRI business competitions by giving some improvement. Yet we stopped in the semifinal. A week later, one of my team mate, saw UT INTECH competition and we again tried to propose the same idea and evaluated it based on prev competition feedback. Long story short, we were given some fund to create the website. We stayed up late almost everyday, even during weekend. Finally we made it :) Even though we only celebrated online, it was really heart warming :)
