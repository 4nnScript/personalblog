Education

Because everything starts with learning

SMA Negeri 3 Malang.

I am going to start from high school as believe me, my formal education story is not that amusing. Anyway, in high school I was not a very active student. SMAN 3 Malang, or we call it Bhawikarsu is the best high school in Malang, it is well known with the intellectual students both academic, and non academic. However, I decided to just enjoy my high school time by hanging out with friends, and studying (lol) to make sure I have a strong foundation of knowledge for the higher education. Even so, I still spared my times to join clubs I love such as japanese club and english club.

The first picture was me and Ryouhei-san, he is Japanese who visited our Japanese club. He did a little presentation about his country. On the next picture, it was taken on 2018 when we just finished performing dance class in PCSC event. PSCS is the biggest event in Bhawikarsu, and my class was chosen to perform in it by doing some theatrical dancing with jugle theme! It was so fun, we trained almost everyday after school until 10pm then I finally got typus :) I know. Great way to ruin the final performance Nisa! However, we still got a great applauds, it feels amazing to be on the stage.
